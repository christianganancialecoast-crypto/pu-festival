About the Event

The PU Festival and Student Day is an annual celebration of student achievements, talents, and campus spirit at Panpacific University. It showcases performances, competitions, exhibitions, and cultural activities that bring together students, faculty, and staff in a spirit of joy and collaboration.

The event highlights not just academic excellence, but also creativity, leadership, and teamwork — embodying the true essence of being a PU student.
